About

South East Engineering Solutions brings deep technical expertise across structural analysis, regulatory documentation, and homologation. Founded by an engineer with roles at Airbus (A350 program), Boeing, Siemens Gamesa, Manor Racing F1, Roborace Formula E, and Xtrac (WRC), we specialize in translating complex engineering challenges into compliance and performance.

We work with innovators across marine technology, electric vehicles, and industrial design — firms that demand rigorous analysis, meticulous documentation, and regulatory precision. Our strength lies in end-to-end delivery: from initial FEA conception through CE marking technical files and homologation sign-off.

Based in Murcia, Spain, we serve clients across Europe and beyond, combining hands-on engineering depth with practical commercial awareness.

Services

Structural FEA

Advanced finite element analysis for tooling, frames, lifting structures, and pressure vessels. We deliver publication-ready stress reports, safety margins, and design optimization across ISO, EN, and client-specific standards.

CE Marking & Technical Documentation

Complete Machinery Directive compliance: risk assessments, technical files, and conformity certificates. Specializing in welded structures, marine equipment, and industrial lifting — ensuring seamless regulatory clearance.

Vehicle Homologation

UNECE and Type-Approval pathway support for novel vehicle architectures. We navigate seatbelt, seating, and powertrain compliance — translating design intent into regulatory submissions.
